# SkillForge - Project Overview

## Vision

SkillForge is an MVP platform designed to empower individuals with financial literacy and career mobility through data-driven insights. By leveraging JAAT (Job Analysis at a Glance) and O*NET (Occupational Information Network) data, SkillForge bridges the gap between educational achievements, current skills, and viable career pathways.

## Value Proposition

### For Students
- **Career Path Discovery**: Explore career options aligned with current skills and educational background
- **Skill Gap Analysis**: Identify missing competencies needed for target roles
- **Financial Planning**: Understand salary expectations and ROI for different career paths
- **Educational Guidance**: Make informed decisions about courses and certifications

### For Workforce Professionals
- **Career Transition Support**: Discover transferable skills for career pivots
- **Upskilling Recommendations**: Identify high-value skills to acquire
- **Market Intelligence**: Access current labor market trends and salary data
- **Personalized Roadmaps**: Get step-by-step plans for career advancement

### For Career Advisors
- **Data-Driven Counseling**: Support clients with evidence-based career guidance
- **Scalable Solutions**: Efficiently serve multiple clients with automated insights
- **Reporting Tools**: Track client progress and outcomes
- **Resource Library**: Access comprehensive occupational and skills data

## Core Problems Addressed

1. **Skills Transparency**: Many individuals don't know how their current skills translate to market opportunities
2. **Career Navigation**: The pathway from education to employment is often unclear
3. **Financial Uncertainty**: Lack of reliable salary and career ROI information
4. **Opportunity Awareness**: Limited visibility into emerging career options and requirements
5. **Decision Paralysis**: Too much information without clear, actionable guidance

## Key Stakeholders

### Primary Users
- **College Students**: Seeking first career or internship opportunities
- **Career Changers**: Mid-career professionals exploring new paths
- **Recent Graduates**: Transitioning from education to workforce
- **Adult Learners**: Returning to workforce or upskilling

### Supporting Stakeholders
- **Career Counselors**: University and professional career advisors
- **Educational Institutions**: Colleges, bootcamps, and training providers
- **Employers**: Companies seeking talent with specific skill profiles
- **Workforce Development Agencies**: Government and non-profit employment services

## Success Metrics

1. **User Engagement**: Active users, session duration, feature adoption rates
2. **Career Outcomes**: Job placements, salary improvements, career transitions
3. **Accuracy**: Relevance of recommendations, user satisfaction scores
4. **Educational Impact**: Course enrollments, certification completions
5. **Platform Growth**: User acquisition, retention, and referral rates

## Differentiation

- **Comprehensive Data Integration**: Combines JAAT and O*NET for rich, validated occupational insights
- **Financial Literacy Focus**: Goes beyond job matching to include salary analysis and career ROI
- **Personalized Pathways**: Tailored recommendations based on individual profiles
- **Actionable Guidance**: Practical steps rather than just information
- **Evidence-Based**: Grounded in authoritative labor market data

## Long-Term Vision

SkillForge aims to become the go-to platform for career planning and development, integrating with educational institutions, employer systems, and certification providers to create a seamless ecosystem for career growth and workforce development.
